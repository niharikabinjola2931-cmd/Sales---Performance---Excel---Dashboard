# Sales Performance Dashboard — Excel

## Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing sales performance 
across countries, products and channels.

## KPIs Tracked
- Total Revenue
- Units Sold
- Net Profit
- Channel Share (Online vs Offline)
- Top Product by Sales
- Best Month
- Top Country

## Charts
- Revenue by Country — Bar chart
- Units Sold by Country — Area chart
- Profit by Products — Horizontal bar chart
- Order Priority Online vs Offline — Donut chart
- Monthly Revenue — Bar chart

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Slicers
- Charts

## Key Insights
- Beverages is the top performing product category
- January recorded highest monthly revenue
- India is the top performing country
- Online channel leads with 54% share
